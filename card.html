Black Wolf

The feet of a wolf will change shape depending on the environment and situation to help the wolf stabilize and maneuver.

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupus
  • Average Weight: 100 - 110 Pounds
  • Average Lifespan: 6-8 Years
  • Habitat: Tundra & Forest Biomes

A black wolf is a melanistic colour variant of the gray wolf. Melanism is a development of the dark-colored pigment melanin, causing the fur of the animal to become a black color.
